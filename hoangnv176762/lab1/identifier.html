Identifiers

Identity Card
An official document or card with someone's name, date of birth, photograph, or other information on it that proves who that person is.
Student Card
A card verifying somebody's identity as a university student and entitling them to services, discounts, etc.
Driver's License
A permit, as one issued by a state's motor vehicle bureau, that allows the holder to drive a motor vehicle on public roads.
Fingerprint
Any unique or distinctive pattern that presents unambiguous evidence of a specific person, substance, disease, etc.
Retina
The innermost coat of the posterior part of the eyeball that receives the image produced by the lens, is continuous with the optic nerve, and consists of several layers, one of which contains the rods and cones that are sensitive to light.
